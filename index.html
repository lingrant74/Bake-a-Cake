Recipe for a Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  • 1/2 cup of wite sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 unsalted butter
    (softened)
  • 1 cup granuated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup buuter milk
    (or use milk with 1 tsp vinegar added)

Instuctions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 8-inch round cake pans
    (or one 9x13 inch pan if you prefer a single-layer cake).

  2. Mix dry ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. Cream the butter and sugar: In a large bowl, beat the softened butter and granulated sugar together with an electric
    mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy (about 3-4 minutes).

  4. Add eggs and vanilla: Add the eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Then mix in the vanilla extract.

  5. Alternate dry ingredients and buttermilk: With the mixer on low speed, add a third of the dry ingredients, followed
    by half of the buttermilk. Repeat the process, ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined – be careful
    not to overmix.

  6. Bake: Divide the batter evenly between the prepared cake pans (or pour all into the 9x13 pan) and smooth the tops.
    Bake for 25-30 minutes (20-25 minutes for a 9x13 pan) or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Cool: Let the cakes cool in the pans for about 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.

  8. Frost and serve: Once cooled, you can frost the cake with your favorite frosting (cheese, buttercream, or chocolate).
    Enjoy!


This cake is soft, moist, and great for any occasion!
